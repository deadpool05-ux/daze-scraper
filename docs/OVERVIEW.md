# Daze Signal Engine Overview

The **Daze Signal Engine** is an elite internal tool developed for **Dazeco**, a premium custom software and AI development agency. It is designed to automate the discovery of high-intent business leads across Reddit and generate authentic, engineering-led outreach content.

## Core Purpose
The engine solves the "cold outreach" problem by identifying potential clients at the exact moment they express a technical pain point or a need for custom development. It bypasses generic lead lists in favor of real-time "signals" from active communities.

## Key Features
- **Multi-Layer Scraping**: Scrapes 4 distinct layers of Reddit (Direct Leads, Intent Leads, AI/Niches, and Regional Pools).
- **Keyword-Based Qualification**: Automatically scores posts based on high-intent keywords (e.g., "budget", "technical debt", "MVP", "scaling").
- **Hybrid AI Engine**: Uses a dual-service architecture (Gemini 2.5 Flash as primary, Groq Llama 3.3 70B as fallback) to generate content.
- **Inbound Authority Posts**: Analyzes top pain points across a subreddit layer to draft a "viral" inbound post that positions Dazeco as a contrarian engineering authority.
- **Automated Comment Drafting**: Generates custom, "no-bullshit" Reddit replies tailored to specific user posts to initiate DMs.

## Philosophy: The "Dazeco Voice"
Unlike traditional sales tools, Daze Signal Engine avoids corporate jargon and AI-isms. It focuses on:
- **Brutal Honesty**: Direct, engineering-first perspective.
- **Ownership**: Advocating for custom, "bare-metal" solutions over bloated SaaS.
- **Authenticity**: Speaking like a human Reddit user (lowercase, abbreviations, no emojis).
