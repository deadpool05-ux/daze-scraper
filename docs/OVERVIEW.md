# Aaditri Signal Engine Overview

The **Aaditri Signal Engine** is an elite internal tool developed for **Aaditri GlobalTech Private Limited**, a premium technology studio. It is designed to automate the discovery of high-intent business leads across Reddit and generate authentic, engineering-led outreach content.

## Core Purpose
The engine identifies potential clients at the exact moment they express technical pain points or a need for custom development in domains like Fintech, AI/ML, and SaaS.

## Key Features
- **Multi-Layer Scraping**: Scrapes 4 distinct layers of Reddit (Direct Leads, Intent Leads, AI/Niches, and Regional Pools).
- **Keyword-Based Qualification**: Automatically scores posts based on high-intent keywords.
- **Hybrid AI Engine**: Uses a dual-service architecture (Gemini 2.5 Flash as primary, Groq Llama 3.3 70B as fallback).
- **Inbound Authority Posts**: Analyzes top pain points to draft viral inbound posts.
- **Automated Comment Drafting**: Generates custom, "no-bullshit" Reddit replies.

## Philosophy: The "Aaditri Voice"
Aaditri GlobalTech is a team of **product builders**, not just service providers. The "Aaditri Voice" leverages the credibility of the products we have built and run ourselves (ExitDebt, myVplayer, Nyomik).

- **Proven Capability**: We prove what we preach by building our own ventures first.
- **Brutal Honesty**: Direct, engineering-first perspective.
- **Ownership**: Advocating for custom, "bare-metal" solutions over bloated SaaS.
- **Authenticity**: Speaking like a human Reddit user (lowercase, abbreviations, no emojis).
